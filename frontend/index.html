FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is food included in the monthly rent?+

Yes! All plans include unlimited breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We serve fresh, hygienic, home-cooked meals prepared daily by our dedicated kitchen staff.

Are both boys and girls accommodated?+

Yes, we have separate wings for boys and girls with dedicated staff, ensuring complete safety and privacy for all residents.

What is the distance from Acharya Institute of Technology?+

MLV PG is located directly opposite Acharya Institute of Technology — just a 2-minute walk from the college main gate. No transport needed!

What is the minimum stay duration?+

The minimum stay is 3 months. However, we offer flexible options for students. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Is there 24/7 security at the PG?+

Absolutely. We have CCTV cameras installed throughout the premises, a security guard on duty 24/7, and a biometric entry system for residents only.
